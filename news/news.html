Welcome to domnere Faculty of Science (FOS)


The Faculty of Science, University of Ruhuna, is committed to excellence in science education, research, and innovation. Since its establishment, the Faculty has played a vital role in advancing scientific knowledge and producing highly skilled graduates who contribute meaningfully to national and global development.

The Faculty offers a dynamic learning environment supported by well-qualified academic staff, modern laboratory facilities, and a strong research culture. Our undergraduate and postgraduate programs are designed to provide a solid foundation in scientific principles while encouraging critical thinking, creativity, and ethical responsibility.

Through teaching, research, and community engagement, the Faculty of Science strives to nurture competent scientists and professionals capable of addressing contemporary challenges and contributing to sustainable development. We warmly welcome students, researchers, and collaborators to be part of our vibrant academic community.

News

32nd Convocation

The 32nd Convocation of the University of Ruhuna was held on December 10th, 11th, and 12th, 2025, at the Rabindranath Tagore Memorial Auditorium.

RISTCON 2025

The 12th Ruhuna International Science and Technology Conference (RISTCON 2025) was held on January 22nd, 2025, at the University of Ruhuna, Matara, Sri Lanka. Organized by the Faculty of Science. It serves as a premier multidisciplinary platform for researchers and academics to share advancements in science and technology.

RUCIT Re-opening

The Ruhuna University Center for Information Technology (RUCIT) recently celebrated its reopening on August 1st, 2025, following an extensive renovation. This modernized facility at the University of Ruhuna serves as a central hub for the institution's digital transformation and IT infrastructure.
